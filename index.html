Method. Each week we crawl Walmart's store-pickup availability for 4 core Doctor's Choice products — Elite-Rx top (navy) & bottom (black), and Pro Fit top (royal blue) & bottom (black) — chosen as the least-likely-to-be-sold-out color of each. A store "carries" a product when it's in stock for in-store pickup. Other colors (gray, pink bottoms, wine, ciel blue) are online-only and never stocked in stores. Counts are a sampled floor; the weekly run expands coverage toward all ~4,788 U.S. stores.
Auto-updated weekly via GitHub Actions.
