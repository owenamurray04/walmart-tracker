How to read this. Data comes from Walmart's store-pickup inventory, checked for all — colorways at all — U.S. Walmart stores (of 4,614 total). A store showing "in stock" is near-certain to carry the item. The reverse isn't: a carrying store can be sold out on check day — so weekly counts are a floor, and the "ever seen stocking" number is the best estimate of true carriage. It rises as weekly runs accumulate.
